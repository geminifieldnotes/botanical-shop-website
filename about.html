Our Origin

How we started; Why we started.

Le Botanical was founded by a team of college students who share the same passion in botany,molecular biology, and plants in general. We have noticed the lack of facilities and locations which hold diverse botanical plant species in our community. Given that botanical plants are essential pieces of medicinal formulation, we wanted to create a sanctuary for these plants for their and our benefit.

We slowly recruited more members to the team, gradually getting bigger by time. We are glad of the individuals who manage Le Botanical and the community we have grown to help and share love for botany with.

Our Mission: Water

Awareness with every plant purchase.

Our actions have triggered a handful of water issues as consequences for irresponsible and unsystematic use of chemicals. These elements pose a threat to our water quality, the marin ecosystem, and livelihood of the people. We are accustomed to think of innovative ideas for the sake of human advantage and less of the resources we deprive.

We are offering our best efforts to contribute in the prevention of further water deterioration. We believe with enough cooperation, we can conquer these issues together.

Our Mission: Plant Restoration

We are just beginning.

Botanical species make up the list of plants declared to be extinct. Althought extinction per se might not always hold a negative note, extinction of specific relevant species must be preserved and cultivated. Thus, the creation of Le Botanical Gardens.

We personally kickstarted the project for medical and academic purposes. We soon realized how much these plants impact the community as a whole and not just the usual target population we initially anticipated.

Upon observation, we were eagered to open our gardens to the public and commercial purposes. We aspire to give customers an experience of seeing a botanical garden personally, with its beauty and glory in the hopes of inspiring them to care more about nature in general.
